About us

ITS Capital AG is an investment company created in 2013 and located in Zurich, Switzerland. We are regulated by FINMA, the Swiss Financial Market Advisory Authority. Additionally, our company is a member of the Swiss Organization for Independent Asset Managers (BOVV). The above allows us to ensure a maximum level of protection and proper professional conduct for the benefit of our clients. But this is not enough for us. We go beyond the legally binding rules and operate as a harbor of trust for our clients.
Wealth for generations
Creation and preservation of wealth needs care. A professional, committed and detail-oriented approach is only the first step towards care. We want that wealth is preserved and multiplied by future generations of the family. This is how we create meaning in our work.
The harbor of trust
We aim to provide an independent, non-biased and personalized approach to all investment decisions. In our opinion, it is important to integrate local short-term targets into global long-lasting solutions. Ultimately, we are a harbor of trust for our clients.
Simplicity and clarity
We aim to simplify complicated matters and bring clarity to all the parties involved so that your financial affairs are in good order and under proper control. In this manner the decision-making process becomes a creative and pleasant exercise. We care and take care, as simple as that.
